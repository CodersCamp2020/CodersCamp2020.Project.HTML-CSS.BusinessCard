I AM W.

Do you want to talk about it?

What is it, this thing about me?

I was an architect for many years, working on complex and multifunctional projects. My job was not only about drawing and designing, but I also covered "the dark side" of architecture - calculations, negotiations with specialists in other fields (like construction, ventilation, electrical), research and presentation. Years of experience tought me how to organize my time and estimate efforts necessary to meet the required deadlines or requirements. Working in Architecture has tought me to never stop learning and never give up on trying to look for a best possible solution. Impossible sometimes just means lack of knowledge or experience. Impossible means searching for freedom.
